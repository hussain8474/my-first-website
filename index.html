MOBHOUSE

Mobiles brand

  • APPLE

    • Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company known for its consumer electronics, software, and services, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, and Apple Watch. They are also known for their operating systems (iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS) and various services like iCloud and Apple TV+. Apple is headquartered in Cupertino, California.

  • SAMSUNG

    • Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate headquartered in Seoul, South Korea, founded in 1938. It is a major player in the global economy, known for its diverse businesses, including electronics, construction, shipbuilding, and more. Samsung Electronics is a key affiliate, specializing in consumer electronics, IT, and mobile communications.

  • REALME

    • Realme is a Chinese smartphone company known for offering feature-rich phones at competitive prices, particularly targeting young consumers. It was initially a sub-brand of Oppo but became independent in 2018. Realme focuses on integrating style and power, emphasizing design and performance. They have expanded beyond smartphones, offering a range of tech accessories and smart home devices.

  • OPPO

    • OPPO is a global smart device innovator, known for its mobile phones, headphones, smartwatches, and other smart devices. They also develop the ColorOS operating system and offer internet services like OPPO Cloud. Founded in 2004, OPPO focuses on combining aesthetic satisfaction with innovative technology, particularly in its Find and Reno series.

  • VIVO

    • Vivo is a Chinese multinational technology company that designs and develops smartphones, accessories, software, and online services. The company is headquartered in Dongguan, Guangdong, and is owned by BBK Electronics. Vivo entered the smartphone market in 2012, initially focusing on the Indian market, and has since expanded to become a global player.
